Methodology

This project evaluates how insurance coverage lapses may affect patient severity and continuity of care.

### Analytical Focus
The dashboard examines:
- prevalence of coverage gaps
- average gap duration
- average severity by gap status
- relationship between gap duration and severity
- gap rate by primary language
- post-gap ER utilization

### KPI Definitions
- Gap Rate = Patients with a coverage gap / Total patients
- Avg. Gap Days = Average number of days without coverage among patients with a gap
- Post-Gap ER Utilization = Percentage of patients with a gap who later used emergency services
- Avg. Severity = Mean severity score by patient group

### Intended Use
This project is designed as a healthcare analytics and decision-support portfolio piece focused on continuity of care, operational risk, and preventable downstream utilization.
