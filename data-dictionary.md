Data Dictionary

- 'patient_id': Unique identifier for each patient
- 'gap_flag': Indicates whether the patient experienced a coverage gap (1 = yes, 0 = no)
- 'gap_days': Number of days the patient was without active coverage
- 'severity_score': Composite score representing patient condition severity
- 'primary_language': Patient's primary language
- 'post_gap_er_utilization': Indicates whether the patient used ER services after a coverage gap
